Height Evolution

Visualizations built using Tableau

The below graph/visualizations is generated by considering the latitude and longitude on the rows and columns field respectively. If we observe the graph, the mean height of every country is described in the visualization using a world map. The visualization gives us a clear idea of the Mean height of different countries with the colors. The lower values start from the green color and gradually increase the opacity of the color till maroon color which represents the higher value of the Mean height. If we observe the map, India has the thick green color which meant that the mean height of India is low likewise we can identify the country with highest Mean height.


In the above graph, we are visualizing the plots of Mean height and Mean height standard error. As we all know, the standard deviation is the variability of the data whereas the mean is the average of the data. Here we are plotting the graphs for these to find the variation of the data around the mean for men and women across the world. As we can observe for men, the standard error has been little closer to the mean line which means that there are no outliers in the data and the variation of the data is also not much. But in case of women, it is different. The standard error and the mean line are little far from each other which means that there are many outliers in the women data. However, we can conclude that the mean height of men is more accurate than that of women.

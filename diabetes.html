Diabetes

Visualizations built using Tableau

Below screenshot represents the Diabetes prevalence world wide. Here we have used the map visualization as data has information of latitude and longitude information and prevalence values for each country. On the right hand side we can see the measure starting the lowest values at 1.75 and the highest are at 20.11. this representation shows the type 2 diabetes.


In line chart , it shows the analysis of how the presence of diabetes disease has increased with time. The scale on x axis shows the year wise split and the scale on y axis shows the diabetes prevalence. On the right side we have interaction like selection of countries list and below there is women men selection. The orange line represents the men and red line is for women. The values are closely twisted but overall the values for both the gender has increased over years. Started at 0.30 to increased at 0.60 at the end of year 2019. The idiom used here is line chart.


The idiom also used here is bubble chart representation, this visualization explains about the country wise Diabetes levels shown using the bubbles. The bubble with large diameter means it has higher values and bubble with smaller diameter shows smaller values. On the right side we have further more interactions like the country selection and gender selection. The countries shown are India, china , Albania, Australia , Bahamas, Bangladesh etc.

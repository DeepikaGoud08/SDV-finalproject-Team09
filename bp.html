Blood Pressure

Visualizations built using Tableau

To know the country wise analysis of the number of the population that are diagnosed with hypertension and what proportion of it is controlled. We used the colour to represent the diagnosed populations and to know whether hypertension got controlled or not is represented by the size of the average measurement of the Proportion of the controlled attribute. We can see that the United States is on the top with more diagnosed and is also the one with a more controlled proportion of 0.4238. Second, we can see Canada with a controlled proportion of 0.41028. Here, controlled proportion means proportion of controlled hypertension out of all the hypertension. We can even filter the data based on the year range and gender to get the various values.


Here, to understand how often a country can diagnose hypertension so that we can get to know the medical measures needed in the future. We used the tableau generated latitude and longitude from the country names we have and coloured the countries based on the proportion of diagnosed hypertension average value - we have values ranging from 0.1429 till 0.7718 mmol/L. So, that the most diagnosed hypertensions can be seen for the countries with highest value of this attribute and are represented by the colour dark green. The colour ranges from yellow to green where yellow being the least and green the most diagnosed countries. The darkest green can be seen for United states of america with the value of 0.7718 mmol/L and the least is seen for Niger 0.1429 mmol/L. From this we can even say that the medical diagnosis in the United states is actually pretty good at diagnosing hypertension whereas in Niger it is not the case. We can use the filters like gender and year wise to get a deeper understanding of the same. These filters were also added.
